PLANT DISEASE DETECTOR

This is a smart AI website to detect the various plant disease . Now a days it is very difficult for a farmer to find out the plant affected by which disease . It is smart helper tool for farmer to detect the disease and gives description about it along with provide link of another wesite for more information about that disease.

Generally this website to detect the plant disease with the help of picture of affected part of plant.Not it initially made such a way to detect the disease through the leaf of the plant .

Instruction :- Take a good picture of the affected part of the plant leaf . Click on choose file and open the picture u have taken .Now click on upload button for the result.
